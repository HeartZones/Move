Setting up the iPad | 3 min

 Guide — 1 Setting up the iPad

First, open the Apple App store on your iPad.  

Search for “Heart Zones Move” in the App Store. 

If it does not appear try adjusting the filter in the upper-left corner to “iPad only”.

Tap download icon and wait for the app to install.

Click the "open" button when it has finished downloading.

Click "Allow" if this option is presented to you.   

Click "OK" to enable turn on your Bluetooth.  This option will not appear if Bluetooth is on.

Click the "Sign Up" button.  

Signing up will remove the unlicensed message (bottom-right) and give you full use of the Move application.
